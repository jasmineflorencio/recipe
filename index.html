Pan-Seared Toast

with Herb Butter Compound

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Nothing beats bread and butter! We use an herb butter compound to elevate this classic American dish to the next level. Take a look at the dark history associated with buttered toast to gain a deeper appreciation for this simple side dish.

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter
  • herb leaves
  • shallot
  • lime juice
  • white-wine vinegar
  • favorite type of bread

Directions

  1. In a nonstick pan, heat 1 tablespoon of butter on medium until melted
  2. Add bread and cook for 3 minutes per side or until golden brown
  3. Optional: Add 1 tablespoon of butter to the pan before flipping for extra buttery toast

To transform your buttered toast into a sandwich, check out Blue Apron's recipe for grilled cheese.
